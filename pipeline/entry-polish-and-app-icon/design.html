Instant Re·Play · Design refinement

Three small refinements.

Autocomplete on the entry fields, a new playwright field, and a home-screen icon. All within the existing Neutra language, nothing new invented. This is the design direction to react to, not the final build.

1 · The entry form

Typing a venue, director, actor, or the new playwright surfaces names you have used before, so the same person is always spelled the same way (which keeps filtering, stats, and export joined up). The suggestion list is a quiet floating plane and is fully keyboard-driven.

The playwright sits just above the director, so the two people who made the production read together. Suggestions come only from your own past entries. Nothing is auto-corrected. If you mean a new name, keep typing and it saves as-is.

2 · In the list

In the log, the playwright appears alongside the director as a matching person. Click the name to filter to everything they wrote; the small boxed icon opens an IMDb search, exactly like director and cast today. Venues stay link-free.

3 · The home-screen icon

When Instant Re-Play is added to an iPhone or iPad home screen, this is the icon it gets. The recommended direction is a little golden-hour proscenium: a warm stage under a soft spotlight, with a quiet aloe play mark at its heart. Theatrical, architectural, and unmistakably ours.
